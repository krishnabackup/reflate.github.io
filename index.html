REFLATE

NATIONAL LEVEL ECONOMICS FEST

ABOUT


REFLATE, is an annual national-level economic fest hosted by the Department of Economics at St. Joseph's College (Autonomous) Devagiri, Calicut, provides students across the country a platform to combine economics learning with competitive and creative expression through a package of exhilarating events and sessions that includes quizzes, paper presentations, debates, simulation games, and lectures by renowned guests. We seek to elicit interest in economics among young minds. Through the years, Reflate has grown in popularity and participation. Reflate has seen participants from reputed institutions all over the nation. And here the next phase of "Reflate" commences.
